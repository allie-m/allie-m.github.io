My name is Allie (she/they), and I am an 18 year old high school senior.

I study at Proof School in downtown San Francisco, and commute by BART from the East Bay every day.

I'm a lover of public transit, and interested in transit-related public policy and local politics.

In my spare time I design maps for fun.

I intend to study graphics and general-purpose GPU programming, and they are the focus of most of my projects.

I have experience using WebGPU/wgpu, Vulkan, OpenGL, OpenCL, and Metal.

I program most often in Rust, but I can also write Python, C, Java, GLSL, MSL (Metal), WGSL (WebGPU), and a little bit of Haskell, Lua, and Javascript.

I sometimes write blog posts about various random things.
