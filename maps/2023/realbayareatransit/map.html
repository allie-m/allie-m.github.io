This map, made in 2023, depicts all of the Bay Area's rail transit as it is projected to in 2024. It consolidates Muni, BART, VTA, Caltrain, SMART, ACE, and Amtrak.

The new Caltrain schedule and reactivation of the L are predicted to happen by the fall of 2024. The rest of the map is as it was when I drew it.

Some, but not all, of the currently proposed projects are included in the map. I arbitrarily chose those which seemed most likely to happen.
