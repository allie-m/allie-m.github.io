This map depicts all of the Bay Area's rail transit as it exists in 2023. It consolidates Muni, BART, VTA, Caltrain, SMART, ACE, and Amtrak's Capitol Corridor and San Joaquins. Longer distance lines (Coast Starlight, California Zephyr, etc.) are not depicted.

I count the L Taraval as part of Muni, even though it's temporarily suspended and replaced with a shuttle bus to West Portal. It's only gonna be like that for another year and there's a continuity of service so whatever I think it's ok.

Some, but not all, of the currently proposed projects are included in the map. I arbitrarily chose those which seemed most close to happening.
