This is a play on my alternate US states map, drawing the states of a 48-state, 7-territory American empire!

I say empire a little facetiously, all the states and territories are co-equal.

This nation is heavily multilingual: in school, everybody must learn English and one other language. Some states mandate there be instruction in their local languages, and the central government encourages this model of linguistic diversity with English as the common language.

There are many unmarked administrative subdivisions, and indigenous groups without territories or states of their own are granted substantial autonomy.

The one group I was a little unsure of how to handle are the Moro. I've decided to copy the solution the modern Philippines has, of giving them a substantially independent territory but keeping them part of the union.
